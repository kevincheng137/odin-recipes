Chinese Meatloaf

Description

Chinese meatloaf is dumpling meat but no bread. Simple and easy to make with less preparation time for a meat based dish.


Ingredients

Most of the measurement of ingredients are simply based on intuition and experience.

Simply add enough of any of the ingredients that do not have a set measurement next to it, but not excessively to ruin the meatloaf.


Required

  • 2 lb Ground beef
  • Moderate amount of Green onion
  • Moderate amount of Ginger
  • Below 1/2 cup Soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 3/4 cup Panko
  • 2 Egg

Optional

  • Small amount of Rice cooking wine
  • Small amount of Sesame oil
  • Enough glaze or sauce to put on top of the meatloaf

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Chop up green onion and ginger
  3. Add all ingredients into large mixing bowl
  4. Mix well enough
  5. Oil a baking pan and put in the meatloaf
  6. Smooth and level the meatloaf evenly
  7. Cut the meatloaf into cubes in the pan so it may bake faster.
    About 3 rows and 6 to 7 columns
  8. Once oven is ready, placed the meatloaf into it and set timer for 50 minutes
  9. Once 50 minutes passed, take out meatloaf,
    cut into the previous cuts you made to separate the meat from each other
  10. Place meatloaf back into the oven and set timer for 30 minutes
  11. Once 30 minutes passed, take out meatloaf and remove excess juices.
    You do not have to remove all the juice
  12. Optional: Put on top of the meatloaf a glaze or sauce
  13. Put the meatloaf back into the oven and set timer for 25 minutes
  14. Once 25 minutes passed, take the meatloaf and remove any more excess juice
  15. It's done
