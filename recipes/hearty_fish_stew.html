Hearty Fish Stew

Description

A hearty fish stew with plenty of ginger for a healthy body. Can be made with any type of fish fillet.


Ingredients

Most of the measurement of ingredients are simply based on intuition and experience.

Simply add enough of any of the ingredients that do not have a set measurement next to it, but not excessively to ruin the meatloaf.


Required

  • Seaweed
  • Fish fillet
  • Ginger
  • Green onion
  • A bit of pepper
  • Soy sauce
  • Fish sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Water

Optional

  • Cooking wine

Steps

  1. Pour enough water to cover the bottom of a wok
  2. Heat the water until boil
  3. While the water is heating up, cut up the green onion and ginger
  4. Put the ingredients in the near boil water
  5. Reduce the heat to medium and steam or boil the fish
  6. Stir the stew so the bottom is not burning and sticking to the bottom of the wok
  7. Once the fish can be broken apart, reduce the heat to low medium
  8. Then after another minute or 3, the stew is ready
