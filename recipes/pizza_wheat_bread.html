Pizza Wheat Bread

Description

A basic bread with cheese and tomato sauce made for pizza.


Ingredients

Most of the measurement of ingredients are simply based on intuition and experience.

Simply add enough of any of the ingredients that do not have a set measurement next to it, but not excessively to ruin the meatloaf.


Required

  • Wheat bread slices
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce

Optional

  • Meat

Steps

  1. Start with 1 slice and pour enough cheese to cover the bread
  2. Pour enough sauce onto the cheese
  3. Sprinkle some more cheese
  4. Place another slice of bread on top
  5. Microwave for 40 seconds
  6. Done
